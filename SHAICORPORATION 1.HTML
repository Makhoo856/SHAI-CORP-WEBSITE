ABOUT US

Shai Corporation is a dynamic and forward-thinking youth owmed company established in 2020 with a clear purpose, which is to contribute meaningfully to reducing unemployment and empowering communities through sustainable business practices. Since its inception, the company has grown into a reliable service provider operating across both public and private sectors. We proudly serve industries such as mining, municipal services, and educational institutions, delivering high-quality products and tailored solutions that meet the evolving needs of our clients. Our diverse portfolio includes the supply of conveyor belt equipment, engineering tools, plant hire services, computer-related products, and corporate branding to name a few. At Shai Corporation, we are committed to excellence, innovation, and integrity. We strive to build lasting partnerships with our clients by consistently delivering value, reliability, and professional service.

MISSION

To provide high-quality products and services that meet and exceed client expectations across mining, municipal, and educational sectors. To empower local communities by creating job opportunities and supporting skills development initiatives. To maintain strong relationships with clients through professionalism, reliability, and exceptional customer service.

VISION

To be a leading and trusted supplier of industrial, technical, and corporate solutions across South Africa, while creating sustainable employment opportunities and contributing to economic growth within our communities.
